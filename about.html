About The Project

Why We Care and You Should Too

Adoptable believes in finding forever homes for every animal.

When the majority of people are looking for a pet, they focus on getting a puppy or kitten, and usually want a certain breed, which means going through a breeder. Even when visiting the shelter for potential pets, many go straight to the babies and pass up the adult cats, black cats, FIV+ ward, seniors, pit bulls, disabled, and animals with chronic conditions. Animals that are often overlooked will remain at shelters for the rest of their lives.

Adoptable is inspired and a tribute to Elmer.

In April 2014, Elmer was shot with a BB gun, while chasing somebody's escaped rabbit. He was brought into Austin Animal Center. Wounded, hurting, and now permanently paralyzed from his lower body, they feared he would not make it and was going to be put down.

The veterinarian chose not to give up on him. He was rehabilitated, cared for, loved, and somehow made it through. He moved into a foster home, who had experience caring for disabled pets. He waited there for two years, and his foster mom began looking into finding a sanctuary that could meet his needs.

One of our developers came across his profile while browsing the Austin Pets Alive site, and considered adopting a differently-abled pet for the first time, due to a video the shelter made to advertise this spunky cat. Elmer found his forever home with Grey and their partner in September 2016, where he is loved and cared for. While adopting Elmer took some time to adjust in the beginning, Grey is so glad they did and plans to adopt plenty more "different" pets in the future! Elmer is the best cat anyone could ask for, and even has his own Instagram.

The mission of adoptable is to help many more people experience the love and joy of these animals who have so much happiness to give.
